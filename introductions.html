Who Am I?

Thanks for taking a look at my portfolio, my name is Andrew Rogers, I live in North Wales (UK) and I've been working as a lecturer in music technology for the past 7 years. I have an honours degree in Music Composition and Technology, a PGCE and I'm 1 assignment away from completing a HND in Software Development through the Open University. I always have a few projects on the go at any particular time, I've built this portfolio site to house the outputs of my interests (or at least the ones that are worth showing). The main threads of my portfolio are education, audio, programming and digital art. This portfolio is new as of February 2016 and I hope to be adding to it whenever I have anything interesting to share.

If you like what you see here please get in touch. I'm open to collaborations, discussing ideas or helping others with their own projects.

Thanks,
Andy
